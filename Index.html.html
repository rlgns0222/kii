East Aisa

East Asia is the eastern subregion of the Asian continent, which can be defined in either geographical[1] or ethno-cultural[2] terms.[3][4] Geographically and geopolitically, the region constitutes Greater China (Greater China consists of Chinese mainland, Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan), Japan, Mongolia, North Korea and South Korea.
